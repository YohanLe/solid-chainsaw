Adulthood

My adulthood was full of dreams and energy to accomplish and change my life. I grow up seeing poverty and struggles. I believe the one and the only way to success in life is having a good education. I worked hard to get a good education. After graduating college got the chance to find a job and earn money to support myself and my families.Earning money and supporting my families was one of my happiest moment.

I got the chance to come to America - the land of opportunity. I experienced snow and very cold weather in the USA for the first time. Seeing snow fall from the sky as some kind of magic was a very exciting moment for me. There were so many days that I went outside and stood. While I like the snow the cold weather was brutal it's like a bittersweet experience
